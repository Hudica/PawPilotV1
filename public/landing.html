What is PawPilot?

PawPilot is your personal homework assistant designed by students for students. Our application features three simple yet intelligent functionalities:
-Explanation
-Resource Review
-Critical Thinking Promotion
Together, these features empower students to excel in their assignments, regardless of ability, by promoting critical thinking.
